# Skill: ticket-analysis

## Goal

Convert vague or business-facing Salesforce tickets into implementation-ready work.

## Steps

1. Summarize the business request.
2. Rewrite it as a user story.
3. Extract acceptance criteria.
4. Identify in-scope and out-of-scope work.
5. Identify likely impacted Salesforce metadata.
6. Identify assumptions, dependencies, risks, and open questions.
7. Flag missing information before implementation.

## Output

- Summary
- User story
- Acceptance criteria
- Scope
- Metadata likely impacted
- Risks
- Dependencies
- Open questions
